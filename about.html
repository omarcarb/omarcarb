All About Me

I am a user interface and user expereince designer with front-end development experience

My Background

Welcome! I'm a self-taught UI/UX designer with a strong grasp of front-end development - HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Born in East Los Angeles and raised in South Gate, my journey has been defined by a passion for crafting engaging digital experiences.

I discovered my affinity for design and front-end development while studying at East Los Angeles College. The challenges of the 2020 pandemic only fueled my determination to improve my coding skills, further enhancing my capabilities.

Beyond the code and designs, I'm dedicated to forging connections between users and technology. Join me on this journey as I continue exploring the dynamic realm of design and development, blending innovation and creativity to shape tomorrow's digital landscape.

My Goals

My goals are a blend of simplicity and ambition. At the forefront, I'm committed to crafting stunning and accessible designs that resonate with all users of my products. Alongside this, I aim to expand my expertise in computer science and design—whether it's UI or video game design—while continually elevating my skills as a designer.

Why a UI/UX Designer?

The world of UI/UX design has held my passion since my teenage years, back when I used to contribute to crafting video game levels. The fascination of observing users interact with my creations and understanding their usage has been a guiding light throughout my journey.
