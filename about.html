About Me!

Hello! My name is Omar Carbajal.

I am a UI / UX designer and front end developer looking to improve both the design and functionality of the web at large,. Here you will get to learn a little bit about me and why I got into this line of work in the first place

Why I chose UI / UX design and front end development?

Designing things has always been a personal passion of mine. Although I wouldn’t consider myself the best at art, I do pride myself in the things that I have been able to create. On top of that, computers, and most importantly computer programming, has been something that I have been really fond of since I was a teenager. So mixing the two together to improve the overall look and feel of a website seemed like a right fit for me. And thus, I began studying UI / UX design and front end development.
